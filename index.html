Bio

A French composer specializing in music for picture and sound design. My journey began with composition for Pokémon fan games, which quickly focused my interest on auditory storytelling. I formalized this passion by earning a Bachelor's degree in Video Game Music and Composition for Picture at the University of Sherbrooke in 2024. During this period, I developed solid expertise through varied projects (orchestral, electroacoustic, sound design) and collaborated with renowned composer Robert Marcel Lepage.

Currently, I serve as Audio Director on the Pokémon fan game, Project Renaissance, the original soundtrack album of which will soon be available. Following my Canadian studies and degree, I have returned to France. I now work as a freelance composer and sound designer. My goal is to bring a creative and technically sharp approach to diverse visual projects—be they films, video games, or trailers—by translating emotion and narrative through sound.
